Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive before departure?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure time to allow for check-in and boarding procedures.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 12 hours before the scheduled departure time. Please contact our customer service team or visit our office for assistance.

What is the luggage allowance?

Each passenger is allowed one large suitcase (max 23kg) and one carry-on bag. Additional luggage may incur extra charges.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for students with a valid student ID. Please present your ID at the time of booking or check-in.
